Getting Started

Welcome to UGC Prompt Studio! This comprehensive guide will help you create professional UGC video prompts that convert.

What is UGC?

User-Generated Content (UGC) is authentic content created by real customers showcasing products. It's proven to increase conversion rates by up to 79% because viewers trust real people over traditional ads.

Key Benefits:
  • Higher trust and authenticity
  • Better conversion rates
  • Cost-effective marketing
  • Increased social proof
