Getting Started

What is UGC Prompt Studio?

UGC Prompt Studio is an AI-powered tool that helps you create professional User-Generated Content (UGC) video prompts. It analyzes your product, selects appropriate actors, configures visual settings, and generates optimized prompts for AI video generation platforms.

The tool is designed for marketers, content creators, and businesses who want to create authentic, high-converting video advertisements that look like real customer testimonials.

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Have your product image ready (or prepare a detailed description)
  2. Know your target audience
  3. Follow our 5-step guided process: Product → Actor → Visual → Hook → Generate
  4. Copy your generated prompt and use it with AI video tools

The entire process takes just 5-10 minutes!

Do I need any special skills or experience?

No special skills required! UGC Prompt Studio is designed for everyone:

  • Beginners: Follow our guided process and built-in recommendations
  • Marketers: Leverage advanced features and A/B testing capabilities
  • Content Creators: Use custom uploads and detailed customization options

Our tutorial and FAQ sections provide all the guidance you need.

What types of products work best?

UGC Prompt Studio works with virtually any product:

  • Physical Products: Electronics, clothing, beauty, home goods, food
  • Digital Products: Apps, software, online courses, subscriptions
  • Services: Fitness programs, consulting, tools, platforms

The key is having clear product information and knowing your target audience.

Product Analysis

Should I upload an image or write a manual description?

Image Upload (Recommended):

  • More accurate and detailed analysis
  • AI captures colors, materials, branding automatically
  • Faster and more convenient
  • Better for physical products

Manual Description:

  • Better control over specific details
  • Good for digital products or services
  • When you don't have high-quality images
  • For emphasizing specific features

What makes a good product image?

For best results, use images that are:

  • High Resolution: At least 800x800 pixels
  • Well-Lit: Natural light or professional lighting
  • Clear Focus: Product is sharp and in focus
  • Clean Background: Minimal distractions
  • Show Key Features: Branding, colors, textures visible
  • Proper Framing: Product fills most of the frame

What file formats and sizes are supported?

Supported Formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, WebP

Maximum File Size: 16MB

Recommended Size: 1024x1024 pixels or larger

If your file is too large, compress it using online tools or image editing software before uploading.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI analysis is highly accurate for:

  • Colors and visual appearance
  • Materials and textures
  • Brand logos and text
  • Product category and type
  • Key visual features

The analysis works best with clear, high-quality images. If results seem inaccurate, try uploading a different image or use the manual description method.

Actors & Performance

How do I choose the right actor for my product?

Consider these factors when selecting an actor:

  • Target Audience Age: Match actor age to your customers
  • Product Relevance: Choose someone who would realistically use your product
  • Lifestyle Alignment: Actor's persona should match your brand
  • Platform Preference: Some actors work better on specific platforms

Popular Choices:

  • Emma (Girl Next Door): Broad appeal, trustworthy
  • Jake (Gym King): Fitness, health, lifestyle products
  • Maya (Gamer Girl): Tech, gaming, Gen-Z products
  • Sarah (Super Mom): Family, household, parenting products

Can I upload my own actor photos?

Yes! You can upload custom actor photos for personalized content.

Requirements:

  • Clear, front-facing photos
  • Good lighting and image quality
  • Appropriate attire for your brand
  • You must have permission to use the photo

Important: Only upload photos you own or have explicit permission to use. Respect privacy and copyright laws.

What's the difference between energy levels and tones?

Energy Levels:

  • Calm: Relaxed, composed presentation
  • Moderate: Balanced, natural energy
  • High: Enthusiastic, dynamic performance

Tones:

  • Conversational: Natural, friendly dialogue
  • Excited: Enthusiastic, upbeat delivery
  • Educational: Informative, instructive approach

Technical Issues

My image upload is failing. What should I do?

Common causes and solutions:

  • File too large: Compress to under 16MB
  • Unsupported format: Use PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, or WebP
  • Poor internet connection: Check your connection and try again
  • Browser issues: Try refreshing the page or using a different browser

If problems persist, switch to the manual description method.

The page is loading slowly or not responding. Help!

Quick fixes:

  1. Refresh the page: Press F5 or Ctrl+R
  2. Clear browser cache: Ctrl+Shift+Delete
  3. Check internet connection: Ensure stable connection
  4. Try different browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
  5. Disable extensions: Ad blockers might interfere

Prompt Generation

How do I use my generated prompt?

Steps to use your prompt:

  1. Copy the entire generated prompt
  2. Open your AI video generation platform (Runway, Pika, etc.)
  3. Paste the prompt exactly as provided
  4. Generate your video
  5. Download and use in your marketing

Important: Don't edit the prompt sections - they're optimized for best results!

Which AI video platforms work with these prompts?

Compatible Platforms:

  • Runway ML: Professional AI video generation
  • Pika Labs: High-quality video creation
  • Stable Video Diffusion: Open-source option
  • Leonardo AI: Creative video generation
  • Most text-to-video tools that accept detailed prompts

Our prompts are formatted to work with any platform that accepts text-based video generation prompts.

Why does my prompt include "DO NOT SUBSTITUTE ANOTHER PRODUCT"?

This instruction is crucial for accuracy:

  • Prevents AI from using similar but different products
  • Ensures your exact product appears in the video
  • Maintains brand consistency and accuracy
  • Protects against generic substitutions

Always keep this instruction in your prompt for best results!

Can I modify the generated prompt?

We recommend using prompts as-is for optimal results, but you can:

  • Safe to modify: Custom dialogue/hook text
  • Avoid changing: Product descriptions, technical specs, actor descriptions
  • For major changes: Generate a new prompt with different settings

If you need different results, it's better to create a new prompt with adjusted settings rather than editing the generated text.
